Transforming Cities Using Space Data

From satellite imagery to actionable insights - our process transforms raw data into sustainable urban solutions.

Data Collection

We gather petabytes of Earth observation data from NASA's satellite constellations, including MODIS, Landsat, and Sentinel missions, providing a comprehensive view of our planet's changing urban landscapes.

AI Processing

Our advanced machine learning algorithms process this data to identify patterns, predict trends, and extract actionable insights about urban growth, environmental changes, and potential risks.

Visualization

Complex data is transformed into intuitive visualizations, interactive maps, and dashboards that make it easy for urban planners to understand and act upon the information.

Decision Support

City officials and planners use our insights to make data-driven decisions about zoning, infrastructure development, green space allocation, and climate resilience strategies.

Sustainable Outcomes

The result is cities that are more sustainable, resilient, and livable - with reduced environmental impact, improved public health, and enhanced quality of life for all residents.
